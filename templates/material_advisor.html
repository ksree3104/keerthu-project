Material Selection Guide

Choose the right material based on your project requirements

Weight Considerations

Aluminium is ideal for applications where weight reduction is critical. Stainless steel provides superior strength-to-weight ratio for demanding applications.

Corrosion Resistance

Both materials offer excellent corrosion resistance, but stainless steel provides superior protection in harsh environments and marine applications.

Cost Analysis

Aluminium typically offers better value for most applications. Stainless steel is recommended when premium quality and long-term durability are priorities.
